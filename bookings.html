How to Book Us

What we need to know
Location
We generally play gigs around the Southeast of England, although we occasionally play further afield (Edinburgh is the furthest we've travelled for a gig). If travel time is greater than a two hour drive, the quote will be increased to include transport costs.
Set duration and Structure
We can play any length of set up to and including two hours. We're happy for this to be in one go or split up into multiple shorter sets.
